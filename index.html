About Us

Established in 1960, Rajeswari Industries is a trusted manufacturer and supplier of Bushes, Temple Bells, and Hand Bells. Based in Ajjaram, Andhra Pradesh, we are known for delivering high-quality products with reliable service. As a leading name in the industry, we specialize in bulk orders, ensuring superior craftsmanship and durability. Choose Rajeswari Industries for the best in quality and service.
